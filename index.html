Our services

Let our reliable management service take care of it for you. Our team experts can complete any tasks quickly and efficiently, saving your time and stress in the long run.

Operational Services

  • Water, fire, mold & smoke restoration or remediation 24/7 emergency response.
  • Save time and stress with our reliable handyman services.
  • Coordinating maintenance and repairs of the property; maintaining maintenance records
  • We are your one stop service platform for renovations(Residential & Commercial)
  • Landscaping, great canadian interlock and stone, Toronto fence, deck & patio team with us
  • Implementation of all government regulations as they apply to the property
  • Monthly building and property inspections; fire plan review, fire safety instructions and emergency response and security review
  • Ensuring compliance with ESA, TSA, HSA and other legal and regulatory requirements
  • High quality commercial, janitorial & office cleaning services.
  • Roofing services, elevate your property with specializing in flat, shigle and metal roofs.

ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICES

  • Enforcement of the Co-operative’s by-laws and policies; Cooperative act and other legal policies to ensure good Co-op governance
  • Promptly and diligently respond to all inquiries or concerns from members and Board of Directors
  • Preparation for and organization of all annual general meetings
  • Monthly manager’s report to the Board and attendance at all regular Board meetings; providing support and training for Board of Directors
  • Developing and implementing a strategy to ensure full occupancy of the Co-op and coordinating new member onboarding
  • Ensuring unit turnovers are completed in a timely manner to minimize vacancy loss
  • Prepare funding applications on behalf the Co-op as required by the Board of Directors
  • Liaising with legal counsel related to the Co-op as required by the Board of Directors

FINANCIAL SERVICES

  • Complete accounting and bookkeeping services
  • Collection of housing charges
  • Preparation of the annual operating and capital budgets
  • Review building conditions assessment report
  • Revenue and expenditure control and analysis
  • Handling receivables and payables, processing and banking
  • Preparing monthly financial statements and arrears reports
  • Facilitating and preparing for the annual audit
